### *Connecting Entrepreneurs. Empowering Leaders. Strengthening Communities.*

Join TABCC's inaugural Business & Leadership Conference for mentoring, networking & the Rhythm & Blues Brunch.

Join the Tuscaloosa Area Black Chamber of Commerce for the inaugural 2026 Business & Leadership Conference, where entrepreneurs, 
professionals, business leaders, and community partners come together to build connections, share ideas, and create opportunities 
for growth. Enjoy inspiring speakers, the Speed Mentoring Power Hour, the Melanin Marketplace Expo, 
a Professional Headshot Lounge, and the Signature Rhythm & Blues Brunch featuring live entertainment and networking. Whether 
you're starting, growing, or scaling a business, you'll leave with valuable relationships, fresh perspectives, 
and resources to help you succeed.
